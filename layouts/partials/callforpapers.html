Submit a talk proposal

Please submit your proposal via this Google form.

SatRday Columbus talks should be about 15 minutes in length and allow for a short Question and Answer guided by one of the conference facilitators. Speakers will present their topic on a live stream. Talks are recorded during the stream and will be uploaded to our conference YouTube channel.

All proposals need to be submitted by August 15, 2021. Accepted speakers will be notified by August 30, 2021.
