Keynote speaker

Nick Wan

Building Virtual Communities: What I learned from sliced

Nick Wan is the Manager of Data Science at Kentucky Fried Chicken and co-host of the competitive data science game show SLICED. He previously worked at the Cincinnati Reds as a Data Scientist. He streams Twitch where he broadcasts SLICED as well as data science projects using data from NFL, MLB, Pro Bullriding Association, Rocket League, Overwatch League, and NWHL. Nick Wan earned his PhD in psychology from Utah State University.
